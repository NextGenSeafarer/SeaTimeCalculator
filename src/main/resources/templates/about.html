As most popularly implemented way to calculate sea-time we use 30 days/month system

Service calculate all days from sign-on to sign-off date inclusively *considering leap years*

It's divided by 30 and remains *if something remains* adding to the result, for example:

161 day will be 5 months and 11 days

Valid dates and vessel name are required *data is collected for statistics only*

Please rate repository if all works fine!
